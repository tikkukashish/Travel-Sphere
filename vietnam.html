Discover Vietnam

Experience the Natural Beauty, Culture, and History of Vietnam

About Vietnam

Vietnam is a country of breathtaking landscapes, from the terraced fields of Sapa to the limestone karsts of Ha Long Bay. It offers a rich blend of history, vibrant markets, and exquisite cuisine that captivates every traveler.

Top Attractions

Ha Long Bay

Known for its emerald waters and towering limestone islands, Ha Long Bay is perfect for a scenic cruise.

Hanoi Old Quarter

Wander through the bustling streets of Hanoi's Old Quarter and experience the unique blend of French colonial and traditional Vietnamese architecture.

Hoi An Ancient Town

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its well-preserved ancient buildings and colorful lantern festivals.

Cu Chi Tunnels

Explore the historic Cu Chi Tunnels to learn about the resilience and ingenuity during the Vietnam War.

Culinary Delights

Vietnam is a haven for food lovers, offering iconic dishes such as pho, banh mi, and fresh spring rolls. Street food markets and traditional restaurants offer a taste of authentic Vietnamese flavors.

Shopping Experiences

Visit local markets such as Ben Thanh Market in Ho Chi Minh City or shop for handmade lanterns and souvenirs in Hoi An.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Tour the Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem Lake in Hanoi
  • Day 2: Take a cruise around Ha Long Bay
  • Day 3: Visit Hoi An and experience the lantern festival
  • Day 4: Explore the Cu Chi Tunnels and enjoy a traditional meal in Ho Chi Minh City

Approximate Budget

Plan your journey with an average budget ranging from 5L to 12L for a week, which covers accommodation, dining, and sightseeing. Premium packages offer more luxurious experiences.
