Explore London

Experience the Heritage, Modernity, and Vibrance of the British Capital

About London

London, the iconic capital of England, is known for its rich history, grand architecture, and dynamic culture. Walk along the River Thames, visit historic palaces, and explore the eclectic neighborhoods that give this city its character.

Top Attractions

Tower Bridge

Walk across this famous bridge and enjoy breathtaking views of the city.

British Museum

Explore artifacts from ancient civilizations and learn about the history of humanity.

London Eye

Take a ride on this giant Ferris wheel for panoramic views of London.

Buckingham Palace

Witness the grandeur of the royal residence and the famous Changing of the Guard ceremony.

Culinary Delights

London's culinary scene is diverse, offering everything from classic fish and chips to high-end dining experiences. Explore street food markets or indulge in afternoon tea.

Shopping Experiences

From luxury brands on Bond Street to unique finds at Camden Market, London has shopping options for everyone.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Tower of London and Tower Bridge
  • Day 2: Explore the British Museum and Covent Garden
  • Day 3: Enjoy the view from the London Eye and stroll along Southbank
  • Day 4: Tour Buckingham Palace and relax in Hyde Park

Approximate Budget

Plan your trip with an average budget of 10L to 20L for a week, covering accommodation, meals, and attractions. Luxury packages are available for more exclusive experiences.
