Discover Los Angeles

Experience the Glitz, Glamour, and Coastal Beauty of LA

About Los Angeles

Los Angeles, the City of Angels, is famed for its entertainment industry, scenic beaches, and diverse culture. Whether you're chasing movie stars in Hollywood or enjoying the sunset in Santa Monica, LA offers an unforgettable experience.

Top Attractions

Hollywood Sign

A global icon, the Hollywood Sign represents the glamour and dream-filled energy of LA’s entertainment industry.

Santa Monica Pier

Enjoy carnival rides, street performers, and stunning views of the Pacific Ocean at the iconic Santa Monica Pier.

Getty Center

Explore world-class art, impressive architecture, and beautiful gardens at this renowned museum.

Griffith Observatory

Visit Griffith Observatory for breathtaking views of Los Angeles and an up-close look at the stars.

Culinary Delights

Los Angeles is a melting pot of global cuisines. From authentic Mexican tacos to high-end dining experiences in Beverly Hills, LA’s food scene is as diverse as its residents.

Shopping Experiences

Explore upscale shopping on Rodeo Drive, quirky boutiques on Melrose Avenue, and the vibrant Grand Central Market for local goods and produce.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit Hollywood Boulevard and the Hollywood Sign
  • Day 2: Explore Santa Monica Beach and Pier
  • Day 3: Tour the Getty Center and Griffith Observatory
  • Day 4: Stroll through Venice Beach and shop on Rodeo Drive

Approximate Budget

Plan your trip with a budget range from 10L to 20L for a week, which includes accommodation, dining, and attraction entry fees. Luxury packages may include celebrity tours and private experiences.
