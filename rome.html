About Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and brimming with iconic landmarks. From ancient ruins to Renaissance art, Rome’s charm captivates every traveler who walks its storied streets.

Top Attractions

Colosseum

Explore the grandeur of the Colosseum, the ancient amphitheater that once hosted gladiator battles and public spectacles.

Vatican City

Visit St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel to admire Michelangelo’s breathtaking frescoes.

Trevi Fountain

Toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain to ensure your return to Rome and take in its Baroque beauty.

Pantheon

A marvel of ancient engineering, the Pantheon is one of Rome’s best-preserved monuments with a magnificent dome.

Culinary Delights

Rome’s culinary scene is unmatched, with traditional dishes like carbonara, cacio e pepe, and gelato found in trattorias and street markets alike. Enjoy authentic Italian flavors and wine pairings that make each meal memorable.

Shopping Experiences

Shop in style at Via Condotti for luxury fashion, or browse unique artisan goods in the vibrant Campo de’ Fiori market.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Visit the Colosseum and Roman Forum
  • Day 2: Tour Vatican City and St. Peter’s Basilica
  • Day 3: Explore the Pantheon and toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain
  • Day 4: Wander through Trastevere and enjoy traditional Italian dining

Approximate Budget

Plan your adventure with a budget range from 10L to 20L for a week, covering accommodation, dining, and entry fees. High-end packages can include private tours and exclusive experiences.
