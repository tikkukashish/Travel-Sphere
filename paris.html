Discover Paris

Experience the Romance, Charm, and Artistry of the City of Love

About Paris

Paris, the heart of France, is known for its stunning architecture, world-class art galleries, and vibrant culture. Wander through cobblestone streets, dine in exquisite bistros, and explore landmarks that define the spirit of this city.

Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

Climb to the top or enjoy a beautiful view from below. The Eiffel Tower offers a magical panorama, especially at sunset.

Louvre Museum

Home to priceless art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo, the Louvre is a must-see for art enthusiasts.

Notre Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, with intricate stained-glass windows and a remarkable facade.

Montmartre

Visit the artistic haven of Paris with a rich bohemian history and breathtaking views from the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Culinary Delights

Paris is a food lover’s paradise! From fresh croissants and delicate pastries to exquisite cheeses and wines, the culinary experiences are endless. Treat yourself to Michelin-star dining or explore cozy cafes offering authentic French flavors.

Shopping Experiences

Whether you’re looking for luxury fashion on Champs-Élysées or unique finds in the boutique-lined streets of Le Marais, Paris offers something for every shopper.

Suggested Itinerary

  • Day 1: Explore the Eiffel Tower and Seine River Cruise
  • Day 2: Visit the Louvre Museum and enjoy an evening walk in Le Marais
  • Day 3: Discover Montmartre and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  • Day 4: Stroll through the Luxembourg Gardens and dine in Saint-Germain-des-Prés

Approximate Budget

Plan your stay according to your budget. Average costs range from 8L to 15L for a week, including accommodation, dining, and entry fees. Luxury packages offer more exclusive experiences.
